## Introduction

Streaming services are great, but loud ads are not. **Hotstar Ad Muter** is a browser extension that recovers your peace of mind.

---

## How it Works

The extension runs a content script that detects when an ad player is active and automatically toggles the mute state.

---

## Tech Stack

-   **JavaScript**: DOM manipulation.
-   **Manifest V3**: Compliant with modern extension standards.

## Conclusion

A simple hack to improve quality of life for binge-watchers.
