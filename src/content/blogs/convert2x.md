## Introduction

Unit conversion is a common need in data processing. **Convert2x** captures this logic in a clean, dependency-free Node.js package.

---

## Features

-   **Comprehensive Categories**: Temperature, Distance, Weight, Volume, Time, and Speed.
-   **Zero Dependencies**: Keeps your `node_modules` light.
-   **Simple API**: `converter.distance.milesToKilometers(10)`

---

## Tech Stack

-   **Runtime**: Node.js
-   **Distribution**: npm

---

## Conclusion

A utility belt for developers who need reliable conversions without the overhead of heavy libraries.
