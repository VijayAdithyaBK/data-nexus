## Introduction

Finding the right office furniture shouldn't be a chore. **Office Haven** is a modern e-commerce platform designed to make buying premium tables and chairs a seamless experience.

Built with a focus on minimalism and usability, it caters to both customers looking for upgrades and admins managing inventory.

---

## The Problem

Many furniture websites are cluttered, slow, or lack proper mobile responsiveness. On the backend, inventory management often requires separate, clunky tools unrelated to the storefront.

---

## The Solution

Office Haven bridges the gap with a unified solution:
- **For Customers**: A beautiful, responsive interface to browse categories like "Large Office Tables" and "Ergonomic Seating".
- **For Admins**: A settled dashboard to manage orders, products, and invoices securely.

### Key Features

- **Cart & Checkout**: Full shopping cart functionality with quantity adjustments and invoice generation.
- **Admin Dashboard**: Secure login to add/edit products and view customer orders.
- **Search & Filter**: Find the perfect desk by price, category, or name.

---

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React (Vite)
- **State Management**: Zustand
- **Styling**: TailwindCSS
- **Deployment**: Netlify

---

## Conclusion

Office Haven demonstrates how modern frontend tools like React and Tailwind can create professional-grade e-commerce experiences that look good and perform even better.
